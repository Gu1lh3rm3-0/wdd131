My country

Brazil, is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest in the world by land area. Its vast expanse covers incredibly diverse landscapes, from the Amazon Rainforest, the largest green lung on the planet, to the extensive paradisiacal beaches along its coast. But Brazil isn't just about its landscapes. It's the people—a vibrant mix of cultures that have created a unique and lively society. You can see this mix in everything from the food, which is rich and flavorful, to the music, with rhythms like samba that just make you want to dance.
